Why would I choose Gambade?

I've been eyeing cargo bikes since a long time...mostly because we don't own a car. While we live in a city where you can rent a cargo bike, you're limited to the opening times of the rent shop, have to go and get it, bring it back, and you need to plan and schedule everything in advance. So I found myself not using a cargo bike quite as often as I'd like to because of these obstacles.

With Gambade, I hope to replace my current commuter bike and have a cargo bike available as well with a much lower effort to actually use it, so I'll enjoy using a cargo bike much more often.
